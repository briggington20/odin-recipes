Pasta Recipe

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: grated cheese, herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with garlic.
  5. Mix well and season with salt.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated cheese and herbs if desired.
