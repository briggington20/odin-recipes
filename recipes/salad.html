Salad Recipe

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: croutons, cheese, dressing

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce.
  2. Slice the cucumber and dice the tomatoes.
  3. Grate the carrot.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and carrot.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss the salad to mix all ingredients.
  7. Add croutons, cheese, or dressing if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your salad!
